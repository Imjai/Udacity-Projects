Blue Whale

Despite being so massive, this giant of the ocean feeds on some of the smallest marine life, tiny shrimp like animals called krill

  • Scientific Name: Balaenoptera musculus
  • Average Length: 29.9 metres
  • Average LifeSpan: 80 to 110 years
  • Habitat: Southern Hemisphere, Antarctic

Blue whales are the largest animals ever known to have lived on Earth.
These magnificent marine mammals rule the oceans at up to 100 feet long and upwards of 200 tons.
Their tongues alone can weigh as much as an elephant.
